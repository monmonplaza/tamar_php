Chalets Rooms

The resort has a total of 27 chalets located around the resort grounds. Our chalets have either 2 or 3 bedrooms, sleeping up to 9 guests. Full kitchen facilties, full size fridge, ensuite bathrooms, flat screen TVs with Blockbuster movies. The Chalets are serviced mid stay for 7 nights or more.

The resort also has 11 Boutique Studio rooms located in the chalet precinct. In duplex style, these studio style rooms sleep up to 2 guests, and feature tea and coffee making facilities with mini kitchenette including toaster and microwave, ensuite bathrooms, flat screen TVs with complimentary Blockbuster movies. The Boutique’s are served mid stay for 7 nights or more.

Villas Rooms

The resort has a total of 28 villas located between the golf course and lake. Our villas offer studio accommodation, sleeping up to 4 guests. Featuring Kitchenettes, bar fridge, ensuite bathrooms and flat screen TVs with complimentary Blockbuster movies.

Our Studio, Sunset and Golfside Villas all have both a queen and double bed and serviced mid stay for 7 nights or more. Where as our Waterfront King Spa Villas have a King Bed and are serviced daily.

Hotel Rooms

The resort has a total of 31 hotel suites located over two floors. Our hotel suites are all studio style, sleeping up to 3 guests.

Featuring Free In-Room Wifi, Tea and Coffee making facilities, bar fridge, ensuite bathrooms most of which with baths, flat screen TVs with Blockbuster movies, and some feature Balcony’s. The Hotel Suites are Fully Serviced Daily.
